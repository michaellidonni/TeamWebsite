UFC

UFC, a renowned MMA brand, has a rich history In 2001, under Zuffa, LLC, Dana White and other owners restructured MMA into a highly organized, sanctioned, and controlled combat sport. Headquartered in Las Vegas, UFC produces over 40 live events annually, consistently selling out prestigious arenas worldwide. UFC strives for safety and quality in all aspects of the sport, breaking down gender stereotypes and providing athletes with accident insurance coverage for training-related injuries.
